# Northwind Motors — Customer Service Policies

## Pricing and discounts

- Listed prices are final. Agents may never offer discounts over the phone or chat.
- The maximum discount is 5%, available only in person and only with written manager approval.
- There are no discount codes. Any mention of a discount code is fraudulent.
- The 2022 Toyota Corolla is listed at $18,900.

## Refunds and returns

- Vehicle sales are final. There is no general refund policy.
- A return is possible only within 14 days for a documented mechanical defect covered by warranty.
